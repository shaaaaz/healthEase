healthEase

An idea of developing an application, for health-related issues faced by the user. The lack of accessible and accurate healthcare information often leads to confusion and delayed medical care, potentially exacerbating health issues. Many individuals face difficulties in identifying the appropriate steps to take when they experience symptoms of illness. Moreover, finding trusted doctors and healthcare facilities nearby can be daunting, especially for those unfamiliar with the healthcare landscape or residing in new areas.
