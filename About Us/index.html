ABOUT BY

Idea Overview: HealthEase is an innovative healthcare guidance app designed to provide users in India with accurate and personalized health recommendations. The app's core features revolve around symptom assessment, disease suggestions, test recommendations, and connecting users with nearby healthcare providers. The goal is to empower users to take informed steps towards better health management and care.

Key Features:
1.Symptom Assessment : Users can input their symptoms through a series of questions or a search bar . The app employs advanced algorithms to analyze the symptoms and suggest potential diseases or conditions.
2.Disease and Test Recommendations : Based on the reported symptoms, the app provides users with a list of potential diseases or conditions. Each suggested disease comes with recommended medical tests that users can consider for confirmation.
3.Location-Based Services:Users can grant location access to the app to receive personalized recommendations based on their geographic location.
